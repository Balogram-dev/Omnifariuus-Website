About Us

Who We Are

Omnifarious Events and Drinks Services is a premium mobile drinks and cocktail brand based in Ilupeju/Mushin, Lagos. Founded by Asaolu Oluwaseun Oluwagbenga, the brand was born out of a deep passion for creativity, great taste, and memorable service. We specialize in crafting exceptional cocktails and mocktails, managing afterparties, and offering professional drink logistics and rentals that bring class and energy to any event

You Ask, We Answer

Do you serve outside Lagos?

Yes, we do serve outside Lagos for special events. Additional travel and logistics fees may apply depending on the location and event requirements.

Are mocktails available?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of delicious non-alcoholic mocktails that are just as impressive and flavorful as our cocktails.

Do you provide drinkware?

Yes, we provide premium glassware and drinkware as part of our service packages. We also offer rental options for additional requirements.

How early should I contact you?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for regular events, and 4-6 weeks for large events or peak season dates to ensure availability.
